Who We Are
Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission

PiE is committed to educational excellence for every student in the Palo Alto Unified School District (PAUSD). We work in partnership with parents, PAUSD, and the community to raise funds for an education beyond what is possible with public dollars -- strengthening our students' K-12 experience by supporting staff and innovative projects.

 

Vision

We believe that a prosperous society has its roots in a strong, well-funded school system. PiE is a unifying force, bringing together educators, parents, students, and members of the community in supporting our public schools. Not only will PiE continue its primary mission of sustaining vital staff and school projects, but we pledge to be innovative in our fundraising strategies and open to ideas from our entire community. We look to our diverse and talented corps of volunteers to help us achieve excellence and equity in education across our school district. Consistently well-funded schools prepare generations of graduates to meet the challenges and opportunities of our world.

 

Values

  1. Partnership – we join forces with parents, the school district, PTAs, boosters, principals, teachers, students, businesses and the extended community to make PAUSD schools the best they can be.
  2. Equity and Excellence – we are dedicated to the founding ideals of equality as we strive to provide the best educational experience possible.
  3. Academic and Personal Growth – we support schools in their efforts to nourish our children’s intellect, self-expression, social skills and engagement with the larger world.
  4. Broad support – we welcome all donations, large and small, and recognize that broad community involvement sends a powerful message of caring about the growth and future of all children in our public schools.
  5. Volunteer diversity – volunteers from all backgrounds strengthen PiE and our schools.
  6. Integrity – fundraising efforts are conducted with honesty and adherence to the highest degree of ethical standards.
  7. Efficiency – a foundation run primarily by dedicated volunteers like ours maximizes the contribution to schools and minimizes overhead.
  8. Innovation – we are committed to innovation in addressing our District’s funding needs, and to encouraging innovation in teaching.
