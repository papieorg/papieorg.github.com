Board of Directors

 

Assistant Treasurer, Chair, Teacher Grants Committee – Director of Finance and Administration: Jeanne Marzano

Jeanne Marzano has three children in the Palo Alto School District and has been actively involved in the schools in various volunteer activities and positions over the last 8 years. More recently, Jeanne has sat on the PTA Board at Jordan Middle School as Auditor and at Duveneck Elementary School as Auditor, VP of Social and is currently finishing a two year term as Treasurer.

Jeanne received her BS in accounting from Santa Clara University and is a CPA. She currently works as a financial contractor on a part time basis. Jeanne has a child at Duveneck, Jordan and Palo Alto High School.
