Palo Alto Partners in Education (PiE) is a non-profit education foundation dedicated to supporting all Palo Alto public schools. We partner with parents, Palo Alto Unified School District and the community to raise funds to give all students in PAUSD the best education possible.

 

PiE Campaign for 2011-12: Thank you to All Donors!

The 2011-12 PiE Campaign has come to a close and PiE is very grateful to all its donors! Contributions by our donors make an enormous difference for our schools, especially in the face of likely PAUSD budget cuts, enabling enrichment and support for all children throughout our district. Thank you again for supporting our schools through PiE! Your donations have become a crucial part of every school's budget, and our district could not maintain its excellent programs without you.
