Personal Donations

You can make a donation to Partners in Education on an installment plan using your credit card. Our minimum donation for installments is $800, which you may give in up to 8 installments of $100 each, so long as you begin your installments by November 25, 2011. When completing the form, enter the monthly payment amount. For example, enter $100 for a $800 donation total.

If you are interested in a different installment plan, please e-mail donations@papie.org and we can support you in creating your own custom installment plan.

You must create a PayPal account to use PiE’s installment plan.

All contributions are fully tax deductible as allowed by tax law (please see below for PiE's Federal Tax ID number).

We appreciate donations of any amount online. However, if you are joining Leadership Circle (donations of $2,500 or more, click here to see benefits), we encourage check donations to save on processing costs. Or, please add 2% to your donation to cover the cost of credit card processing fees.

 

Suggested total donation amounts are:

$800 (one child), with 2% $816
$1,600 (two children), with 2% $1,632
$2,400 (three children), with 2% $2,442
or any amount that is significant to your family to support schools.

(Click here for additional donation amounts adjusted by 2%)
All donations are warmly welcomed, and those over $50 will be recognized in our Annual Report.
