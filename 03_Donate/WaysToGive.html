Ways to Give

Thank you for your interest in making a donation to Palo Alto Partners in Education!  Your gift will help ensure that Palo Alto children continue to receive an outstanding education.

We ask PAUSD parents for $800 per year, per student in the district.  In addition, donors who give more than $2,500 become members of our Leadership Circle program.  Donations of any size are warmly welcomed, and every dollar makes a difference.

You can double your donation to PiE through a matching gift.  To find out if your employer matches donations, see “Matching Gifts” below.

Unless you request to be anonymous, your donations of over $50 will be acknowledged in the annual report (including any matching gift amount that you secure). Donations of all sizes make a difference and are appreciated!

 

Online Giving Gifts to Palo Alto Partners in Education can be made by following this link to donate online now. Partners in Education accepts Visa and Mastercard. And please consider increasing your donation by 2% to cover the cost of credit card processing. Click here for suggested donation amounts increased by 2%.

Gifts of Securities Gift of securities are warmly encouraged as a win-win solution for donors and for PiE. Depending on IRS rules (please consult your tax attorney), an outright gift of appreciated securities often entitles the donor to an income tax deduction equal to the fair market value of the securities. To transfer stock or securities to PiE, please use this form and also please notify us at: donations@papie.org.
Wire Transfers For specific instructions on wiring funds directly to Palo Alto Partners in Education, please call Kathy Schroeder, Executive Director, at 650-329-3990 or email donations@papie.org.
Matching Gifts Corporate matching gift programs are a great way to double or triple your gift. Click here to see if PiE would qualify for your employer’s matching program or contact PiE donations@papie.org
Other Gifts For information regarding planned giving or other gifts, including gifts in kind, please call Kathy Schroeder, Executive Director, at 650-329-3990 or email donations@papie.org.
