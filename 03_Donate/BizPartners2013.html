Current Business Partners

Partners in Education is thrilled to recognize the following Community Business Partners for 2013. We thank these generous businesses and Realtors for their unwavering support of local schools, and invite other local businesses to join these inaugural partners by making a donation to Partners in Education.

Excellent public schools are the cornerstone of a thriving community, attracting the vibrant, committed, and intellectual population whose civic contributions have so marked Palo Alto, Stanford, and Los Altos Hills throughout their history. Consistently ranked among the top districts not just in California, but in the United States, PAUSD's outstanding reputation continues to attract these amazing people to the Palo Alto schools area, supporting our home values and helping maintain a distinctively diverse and interesting community.
