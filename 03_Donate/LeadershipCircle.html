Leadership Circle Categories

Leadership Circle honors families who show extraordinary commitment to supporting our schools financially.  Families may qualify for Leadership Circle through an outright gift, by more than one gift during the school year (with all gifts totaling $2,500 or more) or through an outright gift matched by a corporate matching gift (with a combined total of $2,500 or more). 

Please click here to donate now!

Unless you request that your gift(s) be anonymous we will recognize three levels of outstanding commitment to our schools:

Highest Honors ($10,000 and up)

  • Special mention in the annual PiE Report to the Community at the $10,000 and above level
  • Early notification e-mail for middle and high school tours
  • Invitation to Leadership Circle recognition event
  • Invitation to special group event with the PAUSD superintendent
High Honors ($5,000 to $9,999)
  • Special mention in the annual PiE Report to the Community at the $5,000 to $9,999 level
  • Early notification e-mail for middle and high school tours
  • Invitation to Leadership Circle recognition event
  • Invitation to special group event with the PAUSD superintendent
Honors ($2,500 to $4,999)

  • Special mention in the annual PiE Report to the Community
  • Early notification e-mail for middle and high school tours
  • Invitation to Leadership Circle recognition event

Every Leadership Circle gift makes a profound difference for our schools.  Please consider if your family can contribute at this level, and give as generously as you are able. 
Click here to give today.
