How to Give
Ongoing Monthly Donations

Thank you so much for contributing to an outstanding education for PAUSD students. Our suggested ask is $800 per student per year, but your ongoing monthly gift of any amount, from $100 to $1,000 or more, is welcomed and will make a huge impact on our schools. Please fill in the form below to give in ongoing installments online.

You can email us at donations@papie.org to cancel or change your monthly payments at any time. The minimum monthly payment is $100. You must create a PayPal account to use PiE’s installment plan.

We will provide a tax receipt for your annual contribution in January of the following year. Your contribution will be recognized in our annual report based on the preceding school year.
