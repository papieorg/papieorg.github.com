How to Give

Thank you for donating today to Palo Alto Partners in Education!
Your gift will help fuel our kids' dreams.

Our suggested donation is $800 per student. For those of you who can donate $2,500 or more, you will join the hundreds of families in of our Leadership Circle.

Your donations are essential to PAUSD, providing PAUSD the ability to continue to offer programs that are critical to our children's success including: additional classroom support, science enrichments, art, music, student guidance, elective enrichment and college and career counseling. We understand that not everyone can give at our suggested level. Please give what you can as every dollar helps to ensure our children receive the very best education and are inspired to reach their fullest potential!
