Thank you for your interest in making a donation to Palo Alto Partners in Education.  Your gift will help ensure that Palo Alto children continue to receive an outstanding education.

We ask PAUSD parents for $800 per year, per student in the district. This suggested donation is neither a minimum nor a maximum; we're asking you to give what you can but to consider an amount that is significant to your family. If you can give $2,500 or more per family, you will join the hundreds of families in our Leadership Circle who give a premium to public education in PAUSD. Donations of more than $800 can be paid in installments. Every donation is appreciated and well-spent.

You can double your donation to PiE through an employer matching gift. Unless you request to be anonymous, your donations of over $50 will be acknowledged in the annual report (including any matching gift amount that you secure). Donations of all sizes make a difference and are appreciated!

If you would prefer to mail in your donation, print form here and mail as directed. Please consider increasing your donation by 2% to cover the cost of credit card processing. Click here for suggested adjusted donation amounts.
