In the 2008-09 academic year, PiE introduced the Leadership Circle to honor families who show extraordinary commitment to supporting our schools financially. Thanks to the tremendous response from our community to this initiative we received almost $1,000,000 from 187 families who each donated $2,500 or more.   Families may qualify for Leadership Circle through an outright gift, by more than one gift during the school year (with all gifts totaling $2,500 or more) or through an outright gift matched by a corporate matching gift (with a combined total of $2,500 or more). 

Highest Honors ($10,000 and up)

  • Special mention in the annual PiE Report to the Community
  • Early notification e-mail for middle and high school tours
  • Invitation to end of year thank you/recognition cocktail party
  • Invitation to special group event with the PAUSD superintendent
  • Invitation to individual discussion with PAUSD superintendent

High Honors ($5,000 to $9,999)

  • Special mention in the annual PiE Report to the Community
  • Early notification e-mail for middle and high school tours
  • Invitation to end of year thank you/recognition cocktail party
  • Invitation to special group event with the PAUSD superintendent

Honors ($2,500 to $4,999)

  • Special mention in the annual PiE Report to the Community
  • Early notification e-mail for middle and high school tours
  • Invitation to end of year thank you/recognition cocktail party
