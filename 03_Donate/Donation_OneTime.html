Online Donation

Thank you so much for contributing to an outstanding education for PAUSD students. Our suggested ask is $800 per student, but your gift of any amount, from $100 to $10,000 or more, is welcomed and will make a huge impact on our schools. Please fill in the form below to give online. You do not need a PayPal account for a one-time gift via credit card or E-Check. We will welcome an additional 2% contribution to pay for credit card costs if you are able. Thank you! Click here for suggested adjusted donation amounts.

Gifts of more than $50 will be recognized in our annual report unless you request they be anonymous.
