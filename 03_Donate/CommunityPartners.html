How We Help
Community Partners

These days, in California, public funding alone cannot provide the high quality education that many want for this generation of children. To maintain and build upon the academic excellence that draws families to our neighborhoods and keeps property values high, we depend on parents but also on philanthropists and community-minded individuals who might not currently have children enrolled in Palo Alto Unified schools. Grandparents, parents of PAUSD alumni students, neighbors, civic-minded individuals, and local small businesses and foundations all helped PiE achieve its $4.4 million gift last year. With budgets declining and enrollment growing, your help this year will have a bigger impact than ever before..

Our Community Direct Appeal allows non-parent community members to help fund our Schools Campaign and Teacher Grants. Corporate sponsors help by providing matching gifts. And local foundations and businesses can become PiE Business Partners.

A donation to PiE represents both an investment in the future of the children of Palo Alto, and an affirmation of the core community value of an outstanding public education.
