Installment Donations

Thank you so much for contributing to an outstanding education for PAUSD students. Our suggested ask is $800 per student, but your gift of any amount, from $100 to $10,000 or more, is welcomed and will make a huge impact on our schools. Please fill in the form below to give in installments online. Minimum donation per installment is $100. You must have a PayPal account for installment gifts via credit card or E-Check. We will welcome an additional 2% contribution to pay for credit card costs if you are able. Thank you!
