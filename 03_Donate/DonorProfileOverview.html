Donor Profiles: We Give!

Palo Alto Unified is made truly exceptional by the quality of its schools, staff, and the diverse, talented, and caring community that supports it. Below are profiles of a few of our donors, a mere sampling of the civic-minded citizens who come together to support public education by donating to PiE. We invite you to read about why they give to PiE, and ask you to consider joining the thousands of parents, businesses, and community members who make supporting PiE a priority.

For information on how to donate to PiE or join the Leadership Circle, please click on the orange links.
