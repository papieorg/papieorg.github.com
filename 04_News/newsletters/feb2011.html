PiE's Favorite Valentines:  Our Donors and Volunteers!

 

A Heartfelt Thanks from PiE 

 

 

 

PiE extends a warm thank you to all the families who generously donated to this year's Schools Campaign. We are happy to announce that we surpassed our January participation challenge grant goal! More than 300 new families gave to PiE throughout the month, allowing us to receive the final $50,000 matching grant. We are also thrilled to announce that our overall number of donors increased throughout the school district during this year's campaign! 

 

PiE Funds Spectra Art at PAUSD Elementary Schools

 

 

Stay tuned for our big announcement of PiE's Schools Campaign final results at a March PAUSD School Board meeting, on our Facebook page (Like us on Facebook),or on our website. With the continued support of Palo Alto families, PiE provides critical funding for classroom aides, science specialists, and the Spectra Art program in elementary schools; counseling staff, electives enrichment and technology mentoring in middle schools, and college and career counseling and social-emotional health programs at the high school level. These programs help enhance the school experience for all children in our district. Thanks to everyone for your support!

Our Volunteers Fuel Our Schools

  

PiE particularly wishes to extend a thank-you to the hundreds of volunteers who help us support PAUSD schools.  From our outstanding PiE Chairs at every school to the folks who pitch in for everything from PiE walks to concerts to waving signs about PiE on busy streets, we could not achieve our goals without the help of so many wonderful, helpful volunteers. 

 

Interested in serving as a PiE Chair, or helping out the team, for next year's campaign?  Please contact PiE Executive Director Kathy Schroeder at kschroeder@papie.org.  And thanks again to everyone who lent a hand this year!

 


Prospective Parents Tour Palo Alto High Schools in January

 

 On January 12, nearly 200 parents and helpful volunteers gathered at Gunn and Palo Alto High Schools for the PiE High School Tours.  Our thanks to the administrators and staff at the high schools for the chance to take an in-depth look at these schools!


PiE is very grateful to our donors!  All tax receipts  through January 29 have been issued

 

 

As of now, all tax receipts and letters have been issued through January 29, 2011.  If you made a gift to PiE this school year and have not received a receipt, please contact Executive Director Kathy Schroeder at kschroeder@papie.org.  Thank you again for your support of our schools and our kids!
