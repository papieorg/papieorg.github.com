Season's Greetings from Palo Alto Partners in Education
 
On behalf of all of us at PiE, we wish you a wonderful holiday season.
 
We would like to personally thank all of you who have donated to our current campaigns. Thanks to your support, we are within 10% of our 2009-10 campaign goal.

Community-wide support is critical to our success. We warmly welcome donations of any size. Every gift, large and small, will help us reach our goal and benefit our children.
  
To make a donation now and receive a tax benefit for 2009 (donations need to be in by 12/31/2009),  visit PiE Donations
 
Once again, our children, our teachers, and our community thank you for your generosity to PiE.

Happy Holidays!
