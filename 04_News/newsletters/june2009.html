Best Wishes for Summer 2009
Welcome to Palo Alto Partners in Education's (PiE) last online monthly newsletter for the 2008-09 academic year.
 
This special edition features a listing of all staff and teachers honored by the members of our community through PiE's Honor Your Teacher Campaign. We sincerely thank all parents and community members who together donated over $45,000 to this campaign. Your contributions help fund PiE's annual teacher grant program which supports innovation and excellence in the classroom.
 
We send all of you, especially our new graduating seniors at Gunn and Paly, the best wishes for a great summer and sincerely thank you for your tremendous support. 
Our first newsletter for the 2009-10 academic year will come out in September 2009.
