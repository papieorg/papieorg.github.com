December 2010 PiE Newsletter
A brief note of thanks and warm wishes for a wonderful holiday for all!

Thankful Kids, Thankful PiE!

  
As our children explored the deeper meaning of Thanksgiving through the wonderful, PiE-funded Spectra Art program, all of us at PiE geared up for November Donation Days and our largest fundraising challenge of the year, a generous $275,000 challenge grant provided by PiE's Advisory Council and other community leaders.  Parents, administrators, teachers, and students worked hard to remind parents the critical role PiE plays in maintaining the excellence of our schools.

 

 

And our efforts paid off!  Our community earned the full $275,000, bringing us closer to our final fundraising goal for the year. 

 

We are so thankful for all the families and community members who understand the importance of an outstanding public education and who give to PiE to support the staff and programs that make our schools shine. 

 

 

We thank all our supporters for their generosity and wish everyone a very warm and joyous holiday season!

The Tax Year Ends 12/31/2010.  Please make your donation soon!


 

PiE is a 501(c)3 corporation, and donations to PiE are fully tax deductible as permitted by law.  So, if you want take a deduction in your 2010 taxes for your PiE donation, please donate today.  Though the district office will be closed during the winter break, PiE will still be accepting donations.  In addition to donating online, you can drop a check at your school office until December 16 or mail one to:

Partners in Education

P.O. Box 1557

Palo Alto, CA 94302

at any time before the year ends.  The PiE Office is located in the PAUSD District office at 25 Churchill Avenue.  Please email kschroeder@papie.org or call 650-329-3990, if you want to drop off a gift after December 17.
