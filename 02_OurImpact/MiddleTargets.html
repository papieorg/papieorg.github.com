How We Help
Middle School Targets

At the middle school level, PiE funds make more counselors available to more kids, allowing counselors to stay with their student cohorts through all three grades, forming deeper and more meaningful relationships. PiE funding has allowed Jordan to add electives like Chorus and creative writing, JLS to add public speaking, and Terman to offer drama. Mentors at all three schools help teachers leverage technology to maximize teaching time, and next year will mentor teachers in writing and reading.

Middle School Fund Targets:

Student Guidance: Staff and programs for academic guidance, social and emotional development, student connectedness, and grade-level transitions

Elective Enrichment: Teachers and classroom support in courses like music, creative writing, arts, broadcast journalism, and biotechnology

Teacher Coaches: Staff to help foster better writing and reading and mentors who assist teachers in applying technology to curriculum
