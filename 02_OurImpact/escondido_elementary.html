How We Help
Escondido Elementary School

 

"Partners in Education funds significant instructional programs that are essential to the academic and social success of each and every child at Escondido. These programs encourage a well-rounded and balanced elementary experience. We are fortunate in Palo Alto to have the support from parents and community members to maintain this high level of programming."

– Escondido Principal Danae Reynolds

For the 2012-2013 school year, generous gifts from parents and community members have allowed PiE to provide $213,204 for use at Escondido. During this school year, the funds will be used:

  • To pay for the vast majority of the general classroom aide time, so that all children receive multiple hours of vital classroom aide time weekly, allowing teachers to work in small groups and focus on differentiated learning for all students
  • To pay for 100% of the supplemental science instructor, Mary Jo Ricci, so that all students receive at least one 5-6 week unit of experiential, hands-on science instruction
  • To fund our Spectra art teachers, Alejandra Hermandinger and Alex Provo, so that all classrooms benefit from regular art instruction, for multi-dimensional learning, creativity, and confidence
  • To fund part of the reading program, including the specialists time, Kaisey Hake and Alicia Hernandez, so that all children who need help from a reading specialist may be helped at Escondido, both in English and in Spanish
  • Your 2012-13 PiE Chairs at Escondido are Elizabeth Olson, Clarissa Lee, and May Herr. Questions? Please email them at escondido AT SIGN papie.org.
