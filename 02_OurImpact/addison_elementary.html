How We Helps
Addison Elementary School

 

During 2011-2012, generous support from parents and community donors has allowed PiE to provide Addison with $155,763, which is spent on:


- General Classroom Aides, so that all classrooms will get at multiple hours of vital classroom aide time weekly. PiE funds will pay more than three-quarters of this time.

- More time with Bill the Science Guy from the Junior Museum and Zoo, so that all students receive science instruction that is hands-on, experiential, and fun! PiE funds will cover 100% of this program.

- Spectra Art, so that all classrooms benefit from regular art instruction, to build student confidence and artistic expression. PiE funds will pay for nearly all of this program at Addison.

- Staffing for the Imagination Garden during recess, so that children can have access to a quieter place to be outside and peaceful


“I have worked with school districts all over the state of California and no one prepares our children better for the 21st century economy than PAUSD. They offer kids a program that is not only academically rigorous but includes the arts and deep hands-on science instruction. I am deeply grateful to Partners In Education for their major role in making that happen.”

- Jocelyn Garcia Thome, Addison Principal


Your 2011-2012 Addison PiE chairs are Melissa Reller, Jane Rytina, Andrea Helft, Sharon Hoffman, and Victoria Thorp. Questions? Please email addison AT SIGN papie.org.
