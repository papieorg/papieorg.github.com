How We Help
Addison Elementary School

"As state funding has continued to shrink local budgets, the generous donors of PiE have stepped in again and again to allow PAUSD to keep up its tradition of excellence in education. Plain and simple, we could not do what we do at Addison School without PiE and all of you who support it!"

– Jocelyn Garcia-Thome, Addison Principal

During 2012-2013, generous support from parents and community donors has allowed PiE to provide Addison with $173,228, which will be spent on:

  • General Classroom Aides, so that all classrooms will get at multiple hours of vital classroom aide time weekly. Aides are a tangible, integral part of every childâ€™s classroom experience at Addison, and PiE funds will pay more than 90% of this time.
  • More time with Bill the Science Guy from the Junior Museum and Zoo, so that all students receive science instruction that is hands-on, experiential, and fun! PiE funds will cover 100% of this program.
  • Spectra Art, so that all classrooms benefit from regular art instruction, to build student confidence and artistic expression. PiE funds will pay for nearly all of this program at Addison.
  • Staffing for the Imagination Garden during recess, so that children can have access to a quieter place to be outside and peaceful
  • A counseling intern to provide needed social and emotional support and services need by Addison children and families, two days per week
  • Staff to support the popular Khan Academy after school program at Addison

Your 2013-2014 Addison PiE chairs are Susie Levine, Becky Sinclair, Laura Sturino, Kelli Tomlinson, and Tara Wright. Questions? Please email addison AT SIGN papie.org.
