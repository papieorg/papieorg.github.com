PiE Fair Sponsorship Benefits Program

PiE Fair, Saturday, May 1, 2010 at Addison School Campus, Palo Alto
Deadline for sponsorship applications is April 1st, 2010.

The PiE Fair is a traditional style carnival which immediately follows the May Fete Parade through downtown Palo Alto. The Parade and PiE Fair attracts the majority of Palo Alto Schools, pre-schools and child focused organizations and is a wonderful opportunity for local businesses and organizations to support the local education community. Estimated attendance is 3,000+.
