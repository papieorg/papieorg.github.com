PiE Business Partners Program Details

PiE recognizes that local businesses are the backbone of the Palo Alto, Stanford, and Los Altos communities served by the schools of the Palo Alto Unified School District. So that local businesses and Realtors can indicate their support of schools, and also to provide a way to communicate with PAUSD parents and supporters about those businesses that value schools, PiE has created the Business Partners program to encourage businesses to make a donation in support of schools. PiE Business Partners donate between $500 and $10,000 or more annually, and are recognized in a variety of ways.

Categories of Business Partners include:
Summa Cum Laude, for those giving $5,000 or more
Magna Cum Laude, for those giving between $2,500 and $4,999
Cum Laude, for those giving between $1,000 and $2,499, and
Dean’s List, for those giving between $500 and $999

You may give online here and choose "Business Partners" in the fund category. Or, you can mail your check to Partners in Education, P. O. Box 1557, Palo Alto, CA 94302. Or, call the PiE office at 329-3990 to give a credit card over the phone.

Click here for a copy of our Business Partner Brochure. For more information, please email PiE Executive Director Kathy Schroeder at kschroeder@papie.org.

PiE appreciates the support and generosity of local businesses and recognizes our partners as outstanding community members.
