Our Programs
Palo Verde Elementary School

 

 

"Partners in Education (PiE) is a critical part of our success at Palo Verde. PiE support is essential in helping provide a quality education to our students. Our classroom aides provide our students the additional support that is needed in the classroom. The art enrichment and social and emotional programs are important additions to a well-rounded education for every child."

-Anne Brown, Principal, Palo Verde

During the 2012-13 school year, generous donations from parents and community members have allowed PiE to provide more than $152,000 to Palo Verde. These funds will be used to pay for:

  • 100% of the cost of the general classroom instructional assistants, so that every child has one more person to support his/her needs in the classroom. Each classroom gets 200 hours of aide time every year. The aides will not only assist students, but facilitate the classroom so that the students can spend more time in small groups and one-on-one with the teacher
  • 100% of the Spectra Art program, so that every child can have regular access to art lessons, building creativitiy and confidence.
  • 100% of the Project Resilience program, which builds developmental assets and resilience for all students in the upper grades.
  • With a 50% cut in funding for School Improvement from the state of California, PiE funds are essential to continue to fund our 70% reading specialist. Our reading specialist works with every child who needs extra support in reading, by providing the support that enables the whole classroom to tackle more advanced material and progress in reading proficiency
