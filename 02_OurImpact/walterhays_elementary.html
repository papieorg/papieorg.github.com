How We Helps
Walter Hays Elementary School

 

 

For the 2011-2012 school year, generous donations from parents and community members has allowed PiE to provide $176,884 to Hays. These funds are used on campus:

 

- To fund about half of the general classroom aide time, so that all classrooms will get at multiple hours of aide time weekly, facilitating small group and individual learning

- To fund twice-monthly Spectra art classes, so that all classrooms get regular art lessons and the opportunity to build confidence and creativity through artistic expression

- To fund 100% of the supplemental science program with regular visits from the Junior Museum and Zoo, so that all students receive vivid, hands-on science instruction

- To fund 100% of Dr. Benson’s Perceptual Motor program, helping all Hays kids refine large motion skills during these crucial developmental years

 

"We are so grateful to our parents and community for their generous donations to Partners in Education. These funds enable us to provide rich and relevant experiences for our students and truly support our goal of educating the whole child. Each of these programs enhances our children’s understanding of science, art and themselves. With this much needed classroom aide support, teachers can more effectively address the needs of individual students. Thank you for giving so we are able to maintain these dynamic programs for our children!"

- Principal Mary Bussmann
