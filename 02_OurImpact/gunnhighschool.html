How We Help
Gunn High School

 

"Gunn is a special place for our students in learning and teaching because through PIE the Palo Alto community directly supports our efforts to provide incredible opportunities in new courses and post- secondary planning. Gunn students are directly affected by PIE donations--thank you to all our PIE supporters."

– Katya Y. Villalobos, Gunn Principal

For the 2012-13 school year, generous donations to PiE have allowed PiE to provide Gunn with more than $467,000 for additional staff funding, helping to mitigate the effects of budget cuts and financial uncertainty. These funds are used on the Gunn campus to help every student, as follows:

  • Supportive Guidance Staff — Donations to PiE fund a third of Gunn’s general counseling time along with a Guidance tech. The additional time and support allows all counselors to better serve Gunn students, to increase the assistance available for students in need, and to foster a greater sense of connection on campus. PiE funds also pay for Titan 101, Gunn’s revised Freshman Transition Program, to help ensure that all students get a good start at Gunn.
  • Compelling Electives: — PiE gifts help train the teachers working on the engineering track, including Introduction to Engineering and Industrial Design, and Principles of Engineering. PiE funds allow for additional sections of computer science electives. PiE dollars also support sectionals and accompanists in music, and pay for staffing for Gunn’s busy and popular language lab. By supporting these electives PiE donors help Gunn offer its students many opportunities to pursue and hone their interests.
  • College and Career Counseling — PiE funds 100% of Gunn’s dedicated College and Career Specialist, Leighton Lang. PiE dollars have also allowed Gunn to begin the College Pathway program, and supported College MAP and the PIQE program. And of course, PiE contributions to general counseling help make counselors available to support all students’ post-high-school plans, working in tandem with Mr. Lang.

Your 2012-13 Gunn PiE Chairs are Nancy Smith, Asha Guha, Aarti Johri, and Sonya Bradski. Questions about PiE? Please email them at gunn AT SIGN papie.org.
