Schools Campaign

Thanks to the generosity of our donors, PiE dollars have become crucial at PAUSD schools. PiE collects donations from generous families, community members and businesses for funding target areas that reflect school district needs and priorities deemed most impactful by principals and parents as revealed in annual meetings, surveys, and focus groups. PiE uses these funding targets to allow parent and community donors to better understand how their donations will be spent while providing the flexibility for principals and parents to choose how best to use the donated funds on every PAUSD campus. In addition, in case of severe budget cuts, gifts to PiE may also be used to restore key programs and staff that would otherwise be lost.
